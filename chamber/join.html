Membership Benefits

Our memberships vary based off of benefits and services offered to members.

Non Profit

Cost: Free

Basic Listing: Company name in the online directory.

Limited Event Access: Attend some events, often pay-per-event.

Newsletters: Receive basic Chamber communications.

Bronze Level

Cost: $120 Annually

Enhanced Listing: Contact info and brief description in the directory.

Event Discounts: Reduced fees for events.

Networking: Invitations to networking events.

Basic Support:Basic Support: Access to educational resources and consulting.

Silver Level

Cost: $250 Annually

Premium Listing: Logo, website link, and social media in the directory.

Greater Event Access: Free or discounted entry to more events.

Increased Marketing: More exposure in Chamber communications.

Sponsorships: Discounted sponsorship opportunities.

Advanced Support: Comprehensive business support services.

Gold Level

Cost: $600 Annually

Top-Tier Listing: Detailed profile with multimedia in the directory.

Full Event Access: Free access to all events, VIP invitations.

Maximized Marketing: Featured in all marketing channels.

Exclusive Sponsorships: Prime sponsorship opportunities.

Personalized Support: Dedicated account management.

Leadership: Opportunities to influence Chamber policies.
