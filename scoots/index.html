Welcome to Scoots!

Your ultimate destination for exploring the beautiful island of Cozumel on wheels! Whether you're a seasoned explorer or a first-time rider, we have the perfect scooter, ATV or Jeep for you to experience the freedom and excitement of cruising around this tropical paradise.

We have two wonderful locations to check out.

Our first location is near the Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock in el Centro de Cozumel. Our second location is near the Terminal Puerta Maya further south along the coast - a great location for our customers who are porting from their cruise.
